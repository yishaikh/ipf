Our Mission & Vision

The Islamic Peace Foundation is more than just a place of worship. It is a vibrant community hub where individuals and families come together to grow in faith and character. Our website serves as a digital gateway for our congregants to stay informed about prayer schedules, educational programs, and community outreach initiatives.

We strive to foster an environment of peace, inclusivity, and spiritual excellence, guided by the Holy Qur'an and the Sunnah of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH).
